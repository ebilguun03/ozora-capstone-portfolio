The User

User-centered framing for the Ozora experience

This section explains what Ozora is, why the platform matters, and how a user moves through the dining experience. The structure is designed to stay readable even as detailed user research and examples are added later.

What It Is

A smart dining platform for a more coordinated restaurant visit

Ozora is positioned as a digital dining support system that helps customers browse menus, place orders, and interact with restaurant services through a guided interface. This section can later be expanded with personas, audience segments, or scenario-based examples.

Why Use It

Reducing friction in ordering and service communication

The platform addresses common pain points such as slow ordering, inconsistent communication, and fragmented service experiences. As the page evolves, this area can hold user needs, evidence, stakeholder goals, and concise problem statements.

How It Works

Clear steps from discovery to order completion

01

Discover

Users encounter the platform through the restaurant experience and access the dining workflow.

02

Browse

They review menu information, options, and availability in a structured interface.

03

04

Receive
