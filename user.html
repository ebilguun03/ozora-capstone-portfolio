Stakeholder View

The User

When you dine at Ozora, you do not need to guess what is happening with your order, your tab, or your rewards. You sign in once, see your current dining session, watch items move through the order pipeline in real time, track your running total, and review your past visits without waiting for a printed receipt or asking staff for an update.

Who You Are

You are a restaurant guest who wants your dining experience to feel transparent and convenient. You value knowing what you ordered, what it costs so far, whether items are still being prepared, and whether returning to the restaurant earns you something meaningful.

What You Need

You need faster confirmation, less uncertainty, and less friction during a meal. You benefit from seeing one live source of truth for your session instead of relying on memory, paper receipts, or repeated check-ins with staff.

How Ozora Helps You During a Visit

  • You sign in with simple demo credentials and enter the app without a long setup process.
  • You see your dining session start automatically when you open the Live Tab experience.
  • You watch each item appear and change from sent to preparing to served, which gives you immediate feedback on kitchen progress.
  • You see your subtotal, tax, and total update as orders change, so you always know where your bill stands.

How Ozora Helps You Across Visits

  • You can check your loyalty points and understand what rewards you can actually redeem right now.
  • You can see promotions that are targeted to your customer profile instead of searching for offers manually.
  • You can review your receipt history, which helps you remember past orders and gives you a record of previous visits.

Why This Matters for You

Ozora gives you more visibility during a part of the dining experience that is often invisible. Instead of wondering whether your order reached the kitchen, whether your drinks are delayed, or whether your bill is growing faster than expected, you can see those answers directly in the app. That makes your visit feel more informed, more personalized, and easier to manage.
