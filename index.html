Capstone Portfolio

Designing a more structured, user-centered smart dining experience

Ozora Smart Dining Platform is a restaurant ordering and service concept developed during the University of Washington Bothell CSS capstone. This portfolio is organized around the required course goals: showing the project from the user perspective, documenting how the work evolved over time, explaining the research that supported the project, and reflecting on what was learned during the quarter.

Portfolio Navigation

The four pages organize the capstone into the stakeholder view, the developer view, the research process, and the reflective narrative of the experience.
